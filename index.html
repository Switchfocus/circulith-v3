About

Built for the
Greater Region.
Built to last.

Circulith is a Luxembourg-based industrial processor. We use pyrolysis — a thermochemical process in a sealed, oxygen-free reactor — to break down end-of-life tyres into three usable materials.

The economics are simple: those recovered materials have industrial market value. That value funds our operations. Which is why we can collect and process your tyres at zero cost to you.

Process
  • 01
    Collection
    We pick up end-of-life tyres from your site — free, scheduled, and documented.
  • 02
    Shredding
    Tyres are shredded and separated. Steel wire is extracted immediately at this stage.
  • 03
    Pyrolysis
    Rubber granulate enters a sealed reactor. Heat decomposes it into oil vapour and carbon char. Syngas loops back to power the reactor itself.
  • 04
    Recovery & sale
    Certified carbon black, pyrolysis oil, and steel wire are delivered to industrial buyers. Nothing goes to landfill.
